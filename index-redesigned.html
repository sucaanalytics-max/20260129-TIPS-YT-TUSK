üìù Interpretation Guide

Pearson Correlation: Measures linear relationship. |r| > 0.7 = strong, |r| > 0.4 = moderate, |r| < 0.3 = weak
R¬≤ Value: Percentage of stock price variance explained by views. R¬≤ > 0.5 = strong predictive power
P-Value: Statistical significance. p < 0.05 = statistically significant, p < 0.01 = highly significant
Spearman Correlation: Measures monotonic relationship (works better for non-linear patterns). More robust to outliers than Pearson.
